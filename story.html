My Story


It all started one day when I was sitting with my maternal uncle, scrolling through YouTube shorts and watching videos. We stumbled across a video of someone from the Associate Android Developer team visiting someone's home and giving out certificates and appreciation. I was inspired by this and decided to do some research to find out more about Android and how Android apps are made.

I soon discovered that the things we do on our phones and laptops are done by coding, and I became interested in coding and programming languages. My maternal uncle told me about my other cousin maternal uncle is software engineer and he have good experience in software development field. We both contacted him and he suggested that if we wanted to learn coding and explore this field, We should purchase a laptop.

I asked my parents to buy me a laptop so I could learn coding in a smooth way. After waiting for two or three months, I finally got a good laptop and I started learning python first but at that time I am not able to understand their code syntax so I started fully from basic that's why I learnt HTML at the first.After HTML,I learnt CSS and JavaScript.And now,I am proficient in these technolgies. Currently I am learning node.js for backend development

{(Today, I am a successful front-end web developer and the founder of CodeINBlogs.)}

I am now famous and have been featured in several online news outlets. I hope my story inspires others to pursue their dreams and never give up.

— Anshul Soni {Age: 12 Y/O}
